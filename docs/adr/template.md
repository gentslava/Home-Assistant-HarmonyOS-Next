# NNNN. Short decision title

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by `NNNN-title.md` | Deprecated
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What forces are at play? Constraints, requirements, the problem that needs a decision.
State facts, not the conclusion.

## Decision

The choice we made, in active voice: "We use X." Be specific and falsifiable.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and what becomes harder as a result. Include the trade-offs we accept,
not just the upside. Note anything a future contributor (or agent) must keep in mind.

## Alternatives considered

Options we rejected and the one-line reason each lost.
